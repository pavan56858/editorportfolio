About

I'm a video editor who cuts mostly on CapCut — movie edits, fan edits, and short-form content built around pacing, sound, and rhythm. What started as re-cutting favorite movie scenes turned into a habit of studying how shots, music, and transitions work together to build a feeling.

My edits lean on scene-matching cuts, beat-synced transitions, color grading, and layered sound design to give raw or existing footage a cinematic pace. I'm comfortable working across aspect ratios — 16:9 for YouTube and full-length edits, 9:16 for reels and shorts.

I'm always studying new movies, trailers, and edit styles to bring fresh transitions and pacing choices into my own work, and I enjoy collaborating with creators who want their footage to feel like it was cut for the big screen.
